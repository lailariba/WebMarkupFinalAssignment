Haarlem...

...A historically beautiful and bustling city, offers the warmth of a small town and quick access to nearby Amsterdam. Given its reputation as a market town for centuries, Haarlem is easily identified as a major shopping center in The Netherlands and provides tourists with a diverse variety of exciting attractions and shops. Big retail chains in the main street are alternated by cozy, funny and picturesque shops in the small streets around it. The city center is conveniently within walking distance from the majority of attractions in the area, providing easy means of transport to popular sites, and scenic walks on the way!

A beautiful and scenic stroll aside the Spaarne River, overlooking the Grote Kerk in the distance!

Offering visitors a lively cultural scene, this popular Dutch city has a wonderful atmosphere, excellent shopping and dining, and many picturesque tourist sites! Haarlem’s indubitable charm is discovered by an increasing amount of visitors each year, as it is home to an expansive and flavorful culture!

Haarlem's photogenic canals never disappoint! Haarlem: The Hidden Gem of The Netherlands!

Culture

Haarlem is well worth the visit, as it boasts a magnificent variety of shops, companies, and tourist attractions. It’s a very historical city, with lots of picturesque old buildings to see, making tourism an ever-increasing amount of Haarlem’s income. It is also popular with day-trippers from Amsterdam due to its close proximity to the capital.

Museums and Historical Buildings

The Teylers Museum opened its doors in the late 18th century and is the oldest in Holland, making it a must-see for any tourist visiting The Netherlands. The museum has a very wide-ranging collection from paintings and drawings by Michelangelo and Rembrandt, to fossils and old scientific apparatus. The Grote Kerk church is a must-see for any tourist, as it dominates the Grote Markt, the center market square of Haarlem. This square is also the center piece for many of Haarlem’s famous buildings, and is considered the focal point of the city. A market is held here on Mondays and Saturdays.

Stopped by the Grote Markt one Saturday morning - great variety of produce!

Eating and Shopping

If you’re into shopping, a day in Haarlem is a day well spent, as it’s deemed to be the best shopping city in The Netherlands. Haarlem’s center offers a mix of large chain stores, boutiques, specialty shops, and art galleries - satisfying anyone’s shopping needs. The Grote Houtstraat is the major shopping area with all of the recognizable shops. However, if you go to Gierstraat, Kleine Houtstraat has a wide range of smaller shops. If you’re planning to visit Haarlem on your next trip to the Netherlands, make sure to research the city’s culinary culture! Dutch people in general love exotic food and Haarlem is a city where culinary experiences are highly appreciated. The city’s extensive cuisine options provide tourists with a very unique and well-remembered experience. The Jopenkerk is a wonderful brewery/restaurant located in Botermarkt with a very lively atmosphere! The hall is divided into two parts: tables for guests at one side and a brewery on the other. Brinkmann is positioned right on the main square with lovely views of the Grote Kerk church. Ideal ambiance for breakfast and lunch, with an abundance of delicious dutch food and warm drinks!

Haarlem is filled with little cafes at every street you turn!

Local Sports Clubs

  • SPORTS OLYMPIA HAARLEM
    Sports Olympia provides a wide variety of fun sports, such as softball, badminton, and darts. In addition to supplying a plethora of sports, they also offer rental services for parties or meetings.
    Visit Their website!
  • H.C. HAARLEM
    Hockey Club Haarlem, founded in 1932 as a women’s hockey club, is the largest hockey club in Haarlem. This family friendly club now offers recreational hockey inclusively to both boys and girls.
    Visit Their Website!
  • SPORTCLUB HLC
    Hercules Leonidas Combinatie (HLC) sportclub is an association in Haarlem offering all-inclusive gymnastics, running, circus classes, yoga, and badminton. Known for their gymnastics program with over 700 active members, this association offers gymnastics classes almost every day of the week.
    Visit Their Website!
  • SCOPO ATHLETICO
    Located in Haarlem, Scopo Atletico provides a variety of sports and exercise to younger boys and girls. This associations mission is to enforce the usage of their 3 pillars (mind, movement, and motivation)as the basis for all of their lessons they provide.
    Visit Their Website!
  • TRIATHLON VERENIGING HAARLEM IZGS
    Offers a variety of training clinics, such as swimming, running, and cycling, to prepare for triathlons. In addition, they also organize annual club competitions such as run-bike run, and sprint triathlon.
    Visit Their Website!
  • HWA-RANG DRAGON
    Hwa-Rang Dragon is a martial arts club that specializes in taekwondo, Brazilian jiu jitsu, and mudo. This association offers lessons to all ages, and hosts many martial arts events and showcases.
    Visit Their Website!
  • RFC HAARLEM
    Also known as Rugbyclub Haarlem, this is the largest rugby association in Haarlem! This club offers lessons and matches for anyone interested in playing rugby at their clubhouse found in Haarlem.
    Visit Their Website!
  • DSS BASEBALL SOFTBALL
    DSS is a family association with an ambition for baseball and softball in Haarlem. DSS is a “Socially Responsible Association”, meaning that they are a large part of the Haarlem society and actively offers opportunities to everyone who wants to exercise.
    Visit Their Website!
  • HAARLEM KENNEMERLAND
    Haarlem-Kennemerland Football Club, founded in 2010, is a young and thriving football club fit for all ages. They are situated on the old Haarlem complex on the Sportweg, a very historical stadium, and are an extremely ambitious and committed association.
    Visit Their Website!
  • GYMNASTIEKVERENIGING BATO HAARLEM
    Bato, founded in 1896 as a gymnastics and fencing club, has since then broadened its variety of sports to handball, trampoline jumping, and even tug of war. Since the beginning of this century, Bato has specialized in women’s gymnastics and has been very successful in training high level gymnastics.
    Visit Their Website!
  • DELPHIS SPORT&LIFESTYLE
    Delphis offers a variety of sports to women, and their mission is to make every woman feel at home. They distinguish themselves by their homely atmosphere, personal training sessions, and a challenging variety of group lessons led by professional teachers.
    Visit Their Website!
